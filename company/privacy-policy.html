Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Tripon (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use our website, inquire about Bali packages, or book travel services with us — and how we use, store, and safeguard that information.

Who we are

Tripon provides curated Bali travel packages, destination guidance, and booking support. For privacy-related requests, you may contact us through our Contact Us page or WhatsApp at +91 89198 05038.

Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with Tripon, we may collect:

  • Identity & contact details — name, email, phone number, country of residence, and WhatsApp details.
  • Travel preferences — destinations, travel dates, group size, budget range, hotel category, activities, and special requests.
  • Booking information — passport names, flight preferences, room types, dietary needs, and payment confirmation references (we do not store full card numbers on our servers).
  • Communications — messages sent via contact forms, email, or WhatsApp when you request quotes or support.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, collected through standard analytics to improve our website.

How we use your information

We use your data to:

  • Prepare accurate itineraries and price quotations for Bali packages.
  • Confirm reservations with hotels, transfers, activities, and partner suppliers on your behalf.
  • Send booking confirmations, travel vouchers, and important pre-departure updates.
  • Respond to questions, complaints, or changes before and during your trip.
  • Improve our website, offers, and customer experience through aggregated analytics.
  • Send marketing updates only where you have opted in; you may unsubscribe at any time.

Sharing with third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted partners only when necessary to deliver your trip — for example hotels, airlines, activity operators, payment processors, and visa assistance providers. These partners are required to use your data solely to fulfil the services we book for you.

Cookies & website analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure traffic, and improve performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site features.

Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to complete your booking, meet legal or accounting obligations, and resolve disputes. Inquiry data that does not convert to a booking is typically deleted within 24 months unless you ask us to keep it for future trip planning.

Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No online transmission is 100% secure; please avoid sending sensitive payment details through unsecured channels.

Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, or withdraw consent for marketing. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe. Some requests may be limited where we must retain records for legal or booking purposes.

Children

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

For privacy questions or data requests, visit Contact Us or message us on WhatsApp. We aim to acknowledge enquiries within 2 business days.
