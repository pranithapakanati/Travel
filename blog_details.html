The Brazen Head

At over eight hundred years old, The Brazen Head is one of Dublin's most iconic pubs. Tucked near the River Liffey, it blends old brick walls, low wooden ceilings, and live Irish music into an experience that feels deeply rooted in the city's past.The venue has long been a gathering point for writers, revolutionaries, and travelers. Today, it remains a must-visit stop where visitors can enjoy traditional food, hear local stories, and understand why Dublin's pub culture is such an important part of Irish heritage
